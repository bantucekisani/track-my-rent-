Admin Notes

How to read these numbers properly.

  • MRR and ARR are estimated from the current subscription records and plan pricing.
  • Revenue at risk reflects paid subscriptions currently marked past_due.
  • New MRR is based on paid plans started during the current month.
  • If you need exact realized revenue later, add a dedicated subscription payments ledger or invoice model.
