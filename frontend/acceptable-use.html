This Acceptable Use Policy outlines the rules and guidelines for using the Track My Rent platform.

By using Track My Rent, you agree to use the platform responsibly and comply with all applicable laws.

1. Prohibited Activities

Users may not use Track My Rent to:

  • Engage in illegal activities
  • Upload or distribute malicious software or viruses
  • Attempt to gain unauthorized access to other user accounts
  • Use the platform to harass, threaten, or abuse tenants or other users
  • Upload false or misleading information
  • Attempt to disrupt or damage the platform infrastructure

2. Data Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring that the information they enter into the platform is accurate and lawful.

Users must comply with applicable privacy laws when storing tenant information on the platform.

3. System Abuse

Users may not attempt to exploit bugs, vulnerabilities, or system weaknesses for personal gain.

Automated scraping, hacking attempts, or denial-of-service activities are strictly prohibited.

4. Enforcement

Track My Rent reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate this Acceptable Use Policy.

Serious violations may be reported to relevant authorities where required by law.

5. Policy Updates

Track My Rent may update this policy periodically.

Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
